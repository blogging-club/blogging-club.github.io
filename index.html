✍️ About Us

Hi! We are the MIT Blogging Club — a community of writers who believe in putting ideas out into the world. Whether you're a seasoned blogger or have never published a word, you're welcome here. We meet on the 4th floor of CSAIL and keep things fueled with oranges and Diet Coke.
